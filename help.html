Help & FAQ

Need Help?

If you are experiencing any issues or have any questions regarding the online voting process, you’ve come to the right place! Below you will find answers to common questions as well as contact information if you need further assistance.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I register for voting?

To register for voting, click on the "Register" button on the homepage. Fill in your personal details, such as name, email address, and a password.

2. How do I cast my vote?

Log in, navigate to the "Vote" page, select your candidate, and submit your vote.

3. Is my vote anonymous?

Yes, your vote is completely anonymous. We use encryption to ensure your vote cannot be traced back to you.
